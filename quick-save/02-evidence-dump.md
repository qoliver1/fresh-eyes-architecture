## GATE 02: EVIDENCE DUMP (THE "RAW")
**Target Persona:** Active Agent
**Objective:** Capture the "unfiltered truth" (logs, quotes, errors) to create the final payload.

**Action:**
1. **Collect Signal:** Capture the most critical raw data from the session:
    - **Error Logs:** Any terminal errors or tracebacks.
    - **User Quotes:** Any specific constraints or preferences stated by the user.
    - **Breakthroughs:** The specific command or logic that solved a problem.
2. **Assemble Payload:** Combine the **Rapid Delta** (Gate 01) with this **Evidence** into a single file: `/[persona]-quick-payload.md`.
3. **Verify:** Confirm the file has been written and is ready for the Distiller.

**Constraint:**
- Do NOT attempt to update the Brain or User Profile.
- Keep it raw. No curation.
