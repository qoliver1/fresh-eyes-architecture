# Step 2: Thread Condensation
**Objective:** Compress recent history into a high-signal momentum bridge.

**Task:**
1. Review the last 10 messages of the conversation.
2. Synthesize a 3-5 sentence "momentum summary" that captures not just *what* happened, but the *trajectory* of the conversation.
3. Ensure the summary is written as a bridge for the next agent to step across.
