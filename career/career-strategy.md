# Career Strategy: The Side Door Approach
**Purpose:** A tactical playbook for bypassing traditional credentialing and leveraging "Proof of Work" to enter high-level AI engineering roles.

## 🎯 North Star Goal
Transition from "Self-Taught Developer" to "Recognized Agentic Harness Architect" by solving industry-level problems in public and creating a high-signal portfolio.

## 🛠 Tactical Pillars

### 1. Market Reconnaissance (The "Pain" Hunt)
*Goal: Identify the exact problems that top-tier AI labs (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, Meta) are currently struggling with.*
- [ ] **Monitor Research:** Track arXiv and key AI researchers for "Open Problems" in agent orchestration and memory.
- [ ] **Analyze Frameworks:** Study the limitations of current agent frameworks (e.g., AutoGen, CrewAI, LangGraph) to find "friction points" that can be solved more elegantly.
- [ ] **Social Listening:** Monitor X (Twitter) and engineering blogs for complaints or "wish-list" items from senior AI engineers.

### 2. Proof-of-Work Pipeline (The "Bypass" Engine)
*Goal: Convert technical solves into public signals that attract the right attention.*
- [ ] **The "Solve" $\rightarrow$ "Signal" Workflow:**
    - **Step 1:** Solve a specific, high-value industry problem (e.g., state-drift, context-overflow, hydration latency).
    - **Step 2:** Build a clean, documented GitHub repository with a "First-Principles" README.
    - **Step 3:** Create a "Surgical" technical post or video explaining the *why* and the *how*.
    - **Step 4:** Distribute to the engineering community (not the recruiters).

### 3. Authority Building (The "Expert" Persona)
*Goal: Shift from "applicant" to "peer" in the eyes of industry leaders.*
- [ ] **Scientific Contribution:** Explore proposing hypotheses and drafting technical papers or "specifications" for new harness standards.
- [ ] **Public Reasoning:** Use the `developer-journey.md` to show the "Architectural Intuition" behind the builds.
- [ ] **Strategic Networking:** Engage with engineers on a technical level—contribute to their open-source projects or critique their designs with better alternatives.

## 📈 Current Status: "The Build Phase"
Currently focusing on the "Seamless Resume" and "KISS" architecture as the first major proof-of-work. Once the harness is fully stabilized, it becomes the primary case study for the "Side Door" approach.

**Mantra:** *Don't ask for permission. Build the proof. Document the journey. Make it impossible to ignore.*
